Hi. My name is Julie Stuneck. I am a musician, educator, director and soon-to-be software developer. One of the joys in my previous work has been helping people become better musicians by understanding their strengths and then finding music that will challenge them to improve their weak spots. Another joy is the collaborative aspect of making music with other people. I have also had opportunities here and there to dabble in coding and enjoy the creative, problem-solving aspect of it. I see now that all these experiences have been preparing me for a career as a programmer, where the ability to identify a problem and find creative solutions in a collaborative environment is central to they way they work.
